Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Video Quotes App ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Quote text content you enter
  • Watermark/username information
  • Video title preferences

2.2 TikTok Account Information

When you connect your TikTok account, we receive:

  • TikTok username and display name
  • Access tokens for uploading videos on your behalf

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Usage data and interaction with the Service

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Generate video quotes based on your input
  • Upload videos to your TikTok account when requested
  • Improve our Service and user experience
  • Communicate with you about the Service

4. Data Storage

Generated videos are stored temporarily on our servers. TikTok access tokens are stored in your browser session and are not permanently stored on our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with:

  • TikTok: For video uploads. Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy
  • Pixabay: For background videos. Subject to Pixabay's Privacy Policy

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect your TikTok account at any time
  • Opt out of data collection

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
